About

Since 2008, Kevin has been providing expert tax and accounting services to individuals and business owners in southeast Michigan and the United States. Personalized, efficient, and friendly service are to be expected.

As an experienced accountant and financial controller, Kevin can aid in all financial reporting and tax issues. He specializes in solving IRS and state tax issues that may arise from working with other preparers. Kevin is also an Enrolled Agent, granting him unrestricted rights to represent taxpayers before the IRS.

Kevin holds a ?? in accounting information systems and a Master of Science in Accounting, both from Eastern Michigan University.
